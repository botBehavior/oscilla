oscilla. every sample you hear was computed by Rust on your GPU

The synth engine (oscilla-synth) is unit-tested Rust — pitch proven by zero-crossing tests, envelope by monotonicity — compiled to SPIR-V by rust-gpu, transpiled to WGSL, dispatched block-by-block via WebGPU into an AudioWorklet. Play: Z–M white keys, SD GHJ black keys, ± octave.
